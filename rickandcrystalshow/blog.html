Where it started

Youtube and Arcade April 7, 2014

Rick and Crystal have been doing YouTube videos since Feb. 2021. Following the onset of the Pandemic, Rick and Crystal, like many others, were bored. There was nothing to do and nowhere to go so they decided to make their home arcade.Since building their arcade they have loved everything about it, not just the games, but also the beauty of the lights and artwork. They do videos on many topics, most of which will be on Retro Games and Arcade Machines. They share some opinions and others not so much. Check them out for a Man and a Womans perspective on just about everything.
